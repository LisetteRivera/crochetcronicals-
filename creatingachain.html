Let's Make a Chain!

When it comes to starting a crochet project, we first need to start with a chain!

But, what is a chain? Glad you asked! A chain is the foundation that we will be basing our project on

Step One | Slip Knot

When it comes to make a slip knot, you are sort of making a regualr knot. So you need to wrap the yarn around your finger twice (not too tight) then grab that second line under the first one to pull through and ta-da! You have a slip knot!

Step Two | Making The Chain

After creating your slip knot, you then want to place your hook into the loop you just made.

After creating a slip knot, you then want to go on and put you hook through the lopp you have just created.
